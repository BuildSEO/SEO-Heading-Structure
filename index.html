Every fix, section by section

Left = what was built. Right = what it becomes. Row color tells you the fix type.

H2 Clean Panel Lines (and Panel Restoration, Deep Gloss Finish, Swirl Removal, Detail Renewal, Heat Control)→p Decorative taglines. Style them, don't tag them.
H6 Value Protection (and Impact Recovery, Surface Protection, Restore Clarity, Surface Refresh, UV Defense)→p Kicker labels. Same story.

Size check (do this after retagging)

H1 — biggest thing on the page, always
H2 — smaller than H1
H3 — smaller than H2
p (old taglines/kickers) — styled small, they lost their heading tags, now keep them visually small too

Test yourself

Score: 0 / 10 — answered 0

1. Which of these should be a real heading?

Deleting "Ceramic Coating" would remove a section label, so it is a heading (H2). The others are decoration, a number, and a caption sentence: all p.

2. Right under the H1 sits the big tagline "PROTECT YOUR INVESTMENT". Which tag does it get?

It is decoration. Deleting it loses no section. Tag it p and make it look pretty with CSS.

3. "About Us" is an H2. "Our Mission" lives inside that section. What tag?

A heading inside a section sits exactly one level below its parent. H2 parent, H3 child.

4. A section heading is H2. The first heading inside that section can only be...

Levels step down one at a time, never skip. H2 to H4 or H2 to H6 is always a bug. Size is CSS, not the tag.

5. The process step numbers 01, 02, 03, 04 should be tagged...

Numbers are never headings, not alone, not inside heading text. Styled p elements.

6. The card title "01. Experienced And Trusted Team" is an H5 under an H2. The complete fix is...

Two bugs in one element: the H2-to-H5 jump AND the number baked into the heading words. Half a fix is still a bug.

7. Footer column titles (OUR SERVICES, CONTACT US, FOLLOW US) should be...

Apply the test: deleting "FOLLOW US" removes no content section, it is a navigation label. Tag them p, styled bold. Bonus: the H2-to-H4 jump footers always create disappears with them. Headings are for content only.

8. Which title tag follows the formula?

[2-3 top services] in [City, ST] | [Brand]. The third option is keyword stuffing. The fourth lists services the client does not perform, never invent a service.

9. Best rewrite of the H2 "Locally Trusted"?

Place entity, natural sentence, and it sits above the city buttons that prove it. Option 2 is stuffing: the same entity repeated with no grammar.

10. "The Difference in Our Work" fails which test?

The cover-the-page test. A heading must announce its section. Better: "Our Dent Repair Process: From Inspection to Delivery".
